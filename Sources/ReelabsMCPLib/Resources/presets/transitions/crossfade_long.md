# Crossfade Long

A 0.6s crossfade — long enough to feel intentional, reads as a dissolve rather than a cut-with-softener. Use sparingly; most edits don't need this.

**Reach for this when:** passage-of-time, mood shift, transition between scenes or topics.

**Reach for something else when:**
- You want a soft cut that still reads as a cut → `crossfade_short` (0.25s).
- You want the edit to feel snappy → no transition, just a hard cut.
