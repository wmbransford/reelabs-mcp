# Engaging

Alternating push-in and pull-back across consecutive segments. Odd-indexed segments animate `startScale → endScale`; even-indexed segments animate `endScale → startScale`. Keeps long talking heads energetic without feeling fidgety.

**Reach for this when:** social talking-head content, single-speaker video where the frame needs to breathe and long cuts would otherwise feel static.

**Reach for something else when:**
- You want barely-there motion → try `subtle`.
- You want deliberate cuts with no smooth motion → try `hard_cut`.
