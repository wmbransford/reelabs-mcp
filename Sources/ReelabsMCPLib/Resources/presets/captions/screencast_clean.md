# Screencast Clean

Bottom-of-frame captions designed to stay out of the way of a screen recording. Helvetica medium, 8-word groups, position 92 (very close to the bottom edge).

**Pairs with:** `screencast`.

**Reach for something else when:**
- The screen recording has content at the bottom edge → raise to `position: 85` via an inline override.
- You want the captions more prominent → `subtitle` (larger, higher).
