# Interview Attribution

Quiet subtitles designed for two-person interview content. Lower-third position, larger word groups (8 per), no karaoke — attribution comes from the edit itself (who's on screen).

**Pairs with:** `multi_speaker_guided`, `multi_speaker_batch`.

**Reach for something else when:**
- You want per-word karaoke animation → any preset with `highlightColor`.
- The interview is a "highlight moment" clip → `podcast_big` for punchier styling.
