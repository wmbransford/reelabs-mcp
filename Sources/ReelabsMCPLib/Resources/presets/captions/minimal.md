# Minimal

The quietest caption preset. Light Helvetica, no shadow, no karaoke. Sits at 85% from top.

**Reach for this when:** captions should be barely-there — premium-feeling content, luxury product video, contemplative pieces.

**Reach for something else when:**
- Background is bright or busy — captions will disappear without shadow → `subtitle` (with shadow).
- The video will be watched in public feeds where readability needs to be loud → any karaoke preset.
