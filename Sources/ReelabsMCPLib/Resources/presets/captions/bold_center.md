# Bold Center

Oversized center-frame captions with a neon-green karaoke highlight. Two words per group for maximum punch.

**Reach for this when:** short high-energy clips, punchlines, moments that need to land hard.

**Reach for something else when:**
- Content is conversational / long-form → `william` or `subtitle`.
- You want center captions but less aggressive → `podcast_big` (yellow highlight, black weight).
