# Podcast Big

Massive 10% font-height captions, centered, Helvetica black weight with a bright yellow karaoke highlight. Two words per group.

**Pairs with:** `podcast_clip` / the `podcast_style` variant of `multi_speaker_guided`.

**Reach for this when:** podcast highlight clips where the text IS the visual. Static-frame content where caption is the main animation.

**Reach for something else when:**
- The video has real motion or visual interest → smaller captions like `william`.
- You want landscape interview attribution → `interview_attribution`.
