# TikTok

High-contrast white-on-shadow with a gold karaoke highlight. The most generic "social video" caption style — works when you don't want a strong brand identity.

**Reach for something else when:**
- You want a distinctive voice → `william` (cream + burnt orange).
- You need extra-bold for oversized display → `podcast_big` or `bold_center`.
