# Subtitle

Traditional subtitles — bottom of frame, more words per group, no karaoke animation. Quiet and professional.

**Reach for this when:** the captions should support the content rather than be a design element — documentary, educational, serious interview.

**Reach for something else when:**
- You want per-word karaoke → any preset with `highlightColor`.
- The look feels too plain → try `interview_attribution` (slightly larger word groups).
