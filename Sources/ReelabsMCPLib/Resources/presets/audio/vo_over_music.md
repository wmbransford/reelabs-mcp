# Voiceover Over Music

Narration over a soft bed of music. Volume at 0.15 — loud enough to be felt but VO stays center-stage. Supply `musicPath` at invoke time.

**Reach for this when:** narrated slideshows, documentary-style edits, brand intro videos.

**Reach for something else when:**
- Content is dialogue-driven (podcast, interview) → `podcast_bed` (slightly quieter music).
- Pure silence under the voice → `dry`.
